T037|I booked tickets for a show and selected the print at home option. I printed my tickets but they came out very faded and the QR code is barely visible. I am worried the scanner at the venue will not be able to read the faded barcode. My printer is working fine for other documents but the ticket PDF printed poorly. I do not want to be turned away at the entrance.|Faded QR codes can still scan successfully in most cases as the scanners use infrared not just visible light. Before the event try printing again using your printer settings to increase darkness or contrast and print at the highest quality setting. Alternatively try printing from a different printer such as at a library or print shop using the PDF file. You can also use our mobile app or log into your account on your phone and use the digital version of your ticket which displays the QR code on screen and is usually brighter and easier to scan than a printed version. If you arrive at the venue and the printed ticket does not scan ask staff for a manual entry using your booking reference number which will also be printed on your ticket.
T038|I want to know if I can add more tickets to my existing booking for the same event. I originally booked two tickets but now two more friends want to come. I want all four of us to have the same booking reference for convenience and I want to sit together. Is it possible to add seats to my current booking rather than making a separate new booking?|Adding tickets to an existing booking is not always possible as the system treats each booking as a separate transaction. The best approach is to make a new booking for the additional two tickets and then contact our support team with both booking reference numbers requesting adjacent seating. Our seating team can arrange for all four seats to be placed together in the same section. Alternatively you can try booking the two additional tickets directly through the seat map and manually selecting seats adjacent to your original booking if those seats are still available. If the event is fully sold out contact our support team as we sometimes have access to returned or released seats that are not visible on the public booking page.
